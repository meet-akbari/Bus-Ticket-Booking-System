=| This is an Online Bus ticket reservation system which help you to book your tickets at anytime and anywhere by just  one click...!


  +  How to use ?

  1. To start this system just give run command.
  2. There are 5 different options to handle it.
  3. Except Admin option user can easily use all the option by entering their choice.
  4. For search intercity bus press '1' by shown on screen.
  5. Enter your origin and destination from given cities.
  6. Enter your choice by 1.round trip OR 2.one way.
  7. Input travel date for search bus on that date.
  8. System will shown you available buses on that date and all the information related travelling.
  9. If you want to book ticket press 'b' OR 'm' for back to main menu.
 10. For book ticket follow the steps.
 11. For any type of support you can contact us. Choose support > customer support.
 12. Feedback to the system choose support > feedback.
 13. To close the system back to main menu and press '5'.
